Sketchy-Sketch

Hover your mouse over the boxes on the right. Any box you hover over will get filled in!

The boxes are listed in n by n dimensions - so it will always be a square. To increase or decrease the value of n, use the +/- buttons above. If you want to remove the filled boxes, use the "reset" button.

The dimenions of the box are shown below. The maximum allowed value for n is 50.
